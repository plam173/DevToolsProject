Mastering AI Prompt Frameworks

Learn how structured prompt frameworks can turn vague ideas into powerful AI outputs — from storytelling and brainstorming to coding and strategy.

Explore Frameworks

Popular Prompt Frameworks

1. C.R.E.A.T.E.

Context, Role, Expectation, Action, Tone, Example. A framework that helps you guide the AI with clarity and precision.

2. I.D.E.A.

Intent, Details, Examples, Adjustments. Ideal for creative tasks or marketing prompts.

3. R.A.C.E.

Role, Audience, Context, Expectation. Perfect for communication or content strategy prompts.

Examples in Action

Example 1: Marketing Copy

Prompt (I.D.E.A.): Intent: Write a fun email for a coffee brand. Details: Highlight limited-time pumpkin spice latte. Examples: Starbucks Fall campaign tone. Adjustments: Make it more Gen Z-friendly.

Example 2: Technical Writing

Prompt (C.R.E.A.T.E.): Context: Documenting an API. Role: You are a senior technical writer. Expectation: Write a clear, concise intro. Action: Explain to new developers. Tone: Professional but friendly. Example: Stripe API documentation.

Prompting Tips

  • Be explicit — tell the AI what role it plays and who the audience is.
  • Use examples to shape tone and format.
  • Iterate — refine your prompts based on the responses.
  • Ask for alternatives or step-by-step reasoning for clarity.
